Let $X$ be a non-empty compact [[Metric Space]], and let $f:X\to \mathbb{R}$ be a [[Continuity|continuous]] function on a closed interval $[a,b]\subset \mathbb{R}.$ Then there exist $m,M\in X$ such that 
$$
f(m)\leq f(x)\leq f(M) \quad \forall x\in  X
$$
- $f$ achieves both a maximum and a minimum value
